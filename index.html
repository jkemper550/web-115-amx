Sand Blasting

Sand Blasting hood is necessary The stuff will get everywhere. Even with the hood do not forget to where a dust mask as well, the blasting sand will break up small dust and can cause you to get sore throat or bad lung problems.

Blasting Sand

This is the stuff I use, This is a very abrasive media and is available at Menard's. There are other kinds of blasting sands that are less abrasive that you can get from media blasting suppliers. Do NOT use silica sand, it can cause Silicosis.

My sandblaster

My sandblaster I do not have a lot of money so I have to do a lot of this work myself. My sandblaster is not some big fancy machine it is from Sears.

The Paint color

I have always kind of like the stand out colors, this is not a 1973 color. It is a AMC color called BIG BAD ORANGE Paint color code is DDL2114 for the PPG product

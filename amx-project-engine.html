NOW FOR THE ENGINE

Engine is ready to be inspected

All rods and barrings miked out to .001 inch using plastigage. This engine insides are in good shape.

Also at this point it would be good to inspect the freeze plugs, they are easy to get to now and on inspection I found one of them was almost corroded through

The Heads Were Sent in for inspection and re-surfacing

They did not look bad, still sent them in to be checked out

They came back looking great

They did not look bad and did not have to have a lot done, valves got lapped and heads were planed to make them flat. They were warped a little.

These are dogleg heads

These are the dogleg heads, Named because of the shape of the exhaust ports. These have around a 20% better flow than the squair port heads and will be used on this engine
